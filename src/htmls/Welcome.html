Welcome to RightSize Help

 

RightSize is a teaching aid for programs that teach field and applied epidemiology. We hope that it will be useful for trainees as they are learning statistics or as a refresher after they graduate. Of course, we also hope it will be useful in designing more efficient and useful surveys.

After using the program, we hope that you will

  1. be able to calculate useful sample sizes for surveys,

  2. have a solid understanding of the concepts you are using, and

  3. feel good about applying the concepts.

The program is a result of collaborations among the programs in Training in Epidemiology and Applied Public Health Interventions Network (TEPHINET). The trainees and staffs of many programs contributed helpful suggestions and did testing. We especially want to thank the Philippine and Japanese Field Epidemiology Training Programs, and the Uganda, Zimbabwe, and Vietnam Public Health Schools Without Walls.

We hope you find this information useful. Please report any bugs or errors to Mark White. It will be helpful to include the exact numbers you were using and any calculations you made. Suggestions and comments are also most welcome.
