Proportions

"The proportion of women in this room is 80%."

A proportion measures the central tendency of a discrete variable, just as a mean measures a continuous variable.

When the target population is small, you can determine the proportion directly. For example, if there are 20 people in the room, you can count all the people and their determine that 16 (80%) are women. But if there ate 1000 people in the room. the target population is 1000 and it would be tedious and perhaps innacurate to try to count them all.
