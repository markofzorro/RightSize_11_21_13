The Target Population and the Sample

The group of people you want to know about is the target population.

The people you interview are the sample.

If you want to learn how many women are between the ages of and 12 years 45 years in a village of 500 people, the target population is the 500 people. If you interview 20 people and record their names and genders, these 20 people are the sample.

The sample is the part of the target population you measure to make an estimate about the whole target population.
