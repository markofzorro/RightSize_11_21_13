Simple Random Samples

 

This formula assumes that you will collect a simple random sample to estimate a proportion in the target population. This means that each member of the target population will have an equal chance of being chosen for your sample. If this is not true, this formula will not give you the correct estimate.

Step 1. Convert the percentages to proportions. The proportion in the target population is p = 40% = 0.4, so q = 0.06. The reliability coefficient of 95% which corresponds to a proportion of 0.95. The d value of 5% is 0.05.
